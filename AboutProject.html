This project aims to develop a book availability interface for the students of our college as part of an efficient library management system. The system enables students to register themselves through the website's sign-up portal. Upon logging into their accounts, they gain access to a comprehensive search function to find the desired books. The interface provides real-time information regarding the current quantity of a book available in the library, along with the expected date when the latest copy will be reissued. Once they have obtained the necessary details, students can conveniently log out from their sessions. In addition, the website offers additional features such as a compilation of most preferred books and categorization of books based on different genres. By leveraging this website, we aim to streamline the process of searching for books and obtaining information on their availability in the library, ultimately providing students with enhanced ease of access.
